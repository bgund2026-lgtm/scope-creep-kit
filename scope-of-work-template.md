## 4. Revision Limits

> **This is where most scope creep starts. Be explicit.**

### Included Revisions

| Phase | Number of Rounds | What's Included |
|-------|-----------------|-----------------|
| [e.g., Wireframes] | [2] rounds | Layout changes, content placement, navigation structure |
| [e.g., Design Comps] | [2] rounds | Color adjustments, typography tweaks, spacing, image swaps |
| [e.g., Development] | [1] round | Bug fixes, minor text changes (5 items or fewer) |

### What Counts as a Revision vs. a Change Order

**Revision (included):** Tweaks, adjustments, or refinements to an already-approved deliverable within the same phase. Moving a button. Changing a font size. Swapping one stock photo for another.

**Change Order (additional cost):** Anything that adds new pages, new features, new functionality, or fundamentally changes the direction of an approved deliverable. Redesigning after sign-off. Adding an e-commerce section to a brochure site. Rewriting the navigation structure after it's been built.

**Additional revisions beyond the included rounds:** $[125]/hour, billed in 15-minute increments.
